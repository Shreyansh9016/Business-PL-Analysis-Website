Inventory and product tables were joined using SQL LEFT JOINs to ensure no inventory records were lost during integration. 


SELECT
  a.product_id,
  a.demand,
  a.availability,
  b.product_name,
  b.unit_price
FROM Inventory a
LEFT JOIN Products b
ON a.product_id = b.product_id;
